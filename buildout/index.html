Our Mission

We specialize in the placement of technical personnel at all levels within the food manufacturing industry. We work with national and multi-national food manufacturers in retail packaged goods as well as food ingredients.

We serve the following professions:

  • Food Scientists
  • Chemical and Mechanical Engineers
  • Packaging Engineers
  • QA Professionals
  • Regulatory Personnel and People Qualified for Leadership Roles in Operations.
